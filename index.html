Mountain DAO

A CryptoPhunks production, and a new take on public land.

Mission

We are going to buy a mountain.

Starting with one mountain and potentially expanding further, we will remain committed to the goals decentralization & preservation of mountains around the world. This is an experiment in decentralization, Real Estate tokenization, and the power of memes. Together, we will buy a mountain in Virginia!

Mountain 1

For our first mountain, we have selected a beautiful mountain in the state of Virginia, close the charming town of Roanoke.

The location features scenic views, adventurous hiking options, and endless fresh country air. Check out the Zillow listing for more info
